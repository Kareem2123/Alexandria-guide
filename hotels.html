Luxury & permium Hotels (High-end Tourism)


➤Steigenberger Cecil Hotel Alexandria

Located in the city center and close to the Corniche (sea/port/tourist areas) - close to landmarks such as the Bibliotheca Alexandrina and downtown Alexandria
Rate:★★★★
Address: 16 Saad Zaghloul Square, Raml Station, Alexandria.



➤Four Seasons hotel Alexandria at San Stefano

One of the most famous and luxurious hotels in Alexandria, with an excellent location on the Corniche and a private beach and sea view.
Rate:★★★★★
Address: San Stefano, Alexandria



➤Helnan Royal/Antoniades Palace Hotel

popular among visitors for relaxed stays close to the sea. Offers a comfortable, peaceful stay in a seaside. Often included among recommended hotels for visitors to Alexandria looking for beach-side accommodation and a quieter environment compared with city center
Rate:★★★★✰
Address: Montazah area, Alexandria, Egypt (near Montazah seaside / Corniche Road).

Mid-Range & Budget Hotels (Economy Tourism)


➤Tolip Hotel Alexandria

5-star hotel - close to "Stanley Bridge/Peace Theater/Sidi Gaber Station" depending on the location
A good option if you want a hotel where you can relax without the high costs of "very luxurious" hotels
Rate:★★★★✰
Address:Corniche(Roshdy area)



➤Sheraton Montazah Hotel

One of the well-known five-star hotels on the list of distinguished hotels in Alexandria.
It features rooms overlooking the sea
Suitable if you want your stay in the Montazah/resort area away from the hustle and bustle of the city center
Rate:★★★★✰
Address:Corniche Rood,Montazah,Alexandria



➤Semiramis Hotel Alexandria

Suitable for those looking for economical or mid-range accommodation in Alexandria, especially if you're not concerned with high luxury. What's important to you is Semiramis's location close to the sea/Corniche, and a reasonable price.
Excellent location - close to the sea and the Corniche, and easy access to Alexandria's downtown landmarks.
Rate:★★★✰✰
Address:82 Corniche Road - Raml Station, Alexandria, Egypt
